Bandemonium is a community music group based in Herefordshire. We play a wide range of world, country dance and traditional fold music.

Bandemonium are available to play at weddings, parties, ceilidhs (caller provided) and so on. We also play at social and community functions, schools, day centres and charity fund raisers. If you would like Bandemonium to play at your event please contact us.

We are always happy to welcome new members to join us. No audition required - all abilities and any instruments welcome.

We practice regularly in Hereford and we gig all over the county. If you would like to be part of Bandemonium please get in touch.
